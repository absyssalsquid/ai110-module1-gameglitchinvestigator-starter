## 2. How did you use AI as a teammate?

- Which AI tools did you use on this project (for example: ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot)? 
   - Claude and copilot
- Give one example of an AI suggestion that was correct (including what the AI suggested and how you verified the result).
   - To solve the attempts remaining showing outdated info, claude suggested creating a placeholder element `st.empty()`, then replacing it with an info at the end of the script. 
- Give one example of an AI suggestion that was incorrect or misleading (including what the AI suggested and how you verified the result).
  - To solve the issue of the debugger showing outdated data, Claude suggested adding `expanded=len(st.session_state.history) > 0` to the expander. I did not try/verify the result bc it obviously would not do what I wanted.
  - Then it suggested adding `rerun()` at the end of the submit conditional to force rerendering the page. I verified it by submitting inputs and seeing if the history would update (it did), but this fix then caused a different issue: the hints and messages would be immediately erased after they were rendered.
